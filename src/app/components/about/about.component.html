The Philosophy Project!
We the sapiens is a single person led philosophy project whose aim is to bring age old wisdom from different parts of the world under one roof.
It is to pass down all the knowledge humanity has ever gained over the course of its existence to the kids of 2030 in a consumable manner which they find cool and interesting.
Also, part of the project is to revolutionize storytelling methods with the help of VR. We want humans to be able to and relive the moments from human history.
We wish to cover all the legends and tales which time has buried deep down the literature books.
Let's do it!
